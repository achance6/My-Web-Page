About Me

I'm a senior Computer Science student at Rowan University, expected to graduate with a Bachelors of Science in May 2025.

I am currently seeking a career in Software Engineering. On the surface, I'm interested in SWE roles because I like writing code and I like solving problems — but really, these are a means to an end. The solution to a useless problem is still useless, no matter how interesting the problem was to solve. What I'm really after is making people's lives easier.

As a developer, I focus on creating useful features to make users' lives easier as well as maintainable code to make future maintainers' (who may or may not be myself) lives easier. I am currently an intern at Subaru of America, who has given me ample opportunity to contribute to their technologies. Some of my recent projects there include the migration of a large legacy monolith into modern technologies, a custom analytics solution for a WatchOS app, and a green-field service to serve as a model for cloud-ready, secure, and scalable architecture.

I am currently seeking full-time opportunities in Software Engineering and related roles to be started when I graduate . If you think I would be a good fit for a position, please contact me at my email listed at the bottom of this page. I look forward to connecting with you.
